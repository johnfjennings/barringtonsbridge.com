Barrington's Bridge

Introduction

Rarely in the past has any family or individual have such a profound influence on the development of a locality, nurture of a community while providing an immense heritage to a prospering city than that of the Barrington family.

With the turbulent times of the 17th century been left behind, there saw the commencement of the Barrington family in Limerick. Samuel Barrington moved to limerick late 17th century setting up his trade as a clock and chime maker. Not surviving long in Limerick due to ill health, passing away 2 years later, but leaving behind a successive line of clock makers and foundry owners whom held prestigious positions such as sheriff of Limerick and freemen of Limerick city.
